### **Course objectives  课程目标**

After completing this course, you will be able to:  
完成本课程后，您将能够：
- Strategically apply search methods using controlled vocabulary and indexing  
    策略性地使用受控词汇和索引应用搜索方法
- Demonstrate a physical property search using the numeric filter on Compendex or Inspec databases  
    在 Compendex 或 Inspec 数据库中使用数字过滤器演示物理属性搜索
- Analyze your search results across document types, source, author, specific and broad terms, research trends, publication year, funding, sponsor, ICS code & more  
    分析您的搜索结果，包括文档类型、来源、作者、特定和广泛术语、研究趋势、出版年份、资金、赞助商、ICS 代码等
- Keep your research organized by exporting, saving, and sharing results with collaborators  
    通过导出、保存和与合作伙伴共享结果来保持您的研究组织有序
- Get help when needed with direct access to Engineering Village support  
    获取所需帮助，直接访问 Engineering Village 支持
After completing this lesson, you will be able to:  
完成本课后，您将能够：

- Perform a quick search  
    执行快速搜索
- Utilize pre-defined fields for searching  
    使用预定义字段进行搜索
- Refine your Quick search results  
    优化您的快速搜索结果
